Helena

About

The capital City of Montana, Helena has long since been known to be rich. From the Native tribes that once hunted the plentiful bison, to the prospectors in the year 1864 who struck the jackpot. Helena maintains a strong hold right in the middle of modern day and history. Hikes that go on for miles, a bustling downtown, and plenty of other activities for the individual or for families can be found!

City Facts

  1. Current Population: 33,120
  2. Year of incorporation: 1864
  3. Region: Western
  4. Classification: Urban
  5. Median household income: ~$60,000 (slightly above average)
