Kalispell

About

Kalispell, residing on the doorstep of Flathead Lake and Glacier National Park, this gorgeous mountain attracts the best of the best! Dine in world class restaurants and shop the boutiques in their walkable downtown. Or, load up and go see the crystal clear waters of Flathead Lake. You truly have to see it to believe it!

City Facts

  1. Current Population: 24,558
  2. Year of Incorporation: 1892
  3. Region: Northwest
  4. Classification: Rural
  5. Median household income: $47,750 (lower than state average)
