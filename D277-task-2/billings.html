Billings

About

Boasting the largest population of any city in Montana, Billings is known as, "Montana's Trailhead." As in, it leads you to all the adventure Montana has to offer! Take a hike on the Rims for a breathtaking view of the valley below, hit the river for some incredible fishing, or make your way to many of the nearby mountains!

Along with the beautiful sights, Billings is thriving with a growing dining and craft beer scene. There's something for everyone here!

City Facts

  1. Current Population: 117,445

  2. Year of incorporation: 1882

  3. Region: Southeast

  4. Classification: Urban

  5. Median household income: ~$63,000 (slightly above average)
